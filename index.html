Aim of the Blackjack Game is to get 21 or as close to as possible. Number cards have their face value, jacks, kings and queens are worth 10. Ace can be either 1 or 11 and the player who holds the ace gets to choose the value of the card. The dealer and the player start with two cards. Hit to take another card or stay to compare hands.
